What is Waxing?

This is basically a temporary technique of hair removal like plucking and threading. The good quality of natural wax leaves a glowing and smoothening effect on the skin. The re-growth is sparse and soft unlike in case of shaving. It can be done for legs, arms, back, and abdomen and for whole body.
